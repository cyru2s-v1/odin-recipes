Steak and Eggs

Description

Steak and eggs is a classic dish that typically consists of a grilled or pan-seared steak served alongside eggs, often prepared sunny-side up, over easy, or scrambled. The steak can be seasoned with various herbs and spices and cooked to the desired level of doneness, ranging from rare to well-done. It's a hearty and protein-rich breakfast or brunch option that provides a satisfying combination of flavors and textures. The eggs complement the savory taste of the steak, and the dish is sometimes accompanied by sides such as toast, hash browns, or vegetables.

Ingredients

  • Steak of your choice (Ribeye Preferred)
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Thyme
  • Butter
  • Eggs

Cooking Instructions

  1. Season Steak
  2. Cook Steak
  3. Season Eggs
  4. Cook Eggs
