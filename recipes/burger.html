Burger

Description

A burger, short for hamburger, is a popular and iconic food item consisting of a ground meat patty, typically beef, that is seasoned, cooked, and placed inside a sliced bun. The patty is often grilled, pan-fried, or charbroiled to achieve a flavorful and juicy texture. Burgers are known for their versatility, allowing for a wide range of toppings and condiments to enhance taste and texture.

Ingredients

  • Bun
  • Ground Beef Patty
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Onion
  • Ketchup

Cooking Instructions

  1. Prepare Patty
  2. Cook Patty
  3. Slice Vegetables
  4. Toast Bun
  5. Ketchup on Bun
  6. Assemble Burger
